Blackout Chocolate Cake

The best chocolate cake recipe. There are plenty of claims for the best chocolate cake recipe. I get that. But with one bite of this decadent, moist chocolate cake with chocolate frosting, every single person around the table commented that this was the best chocolate cake they’d ever tasted.



Chocolate Mousse

Don’t let the French name fool you, chocolate mousse is one of the easiest desserts to make. Seriously, if you have a microwave, a hand mixer, and a rubber spatula, you can make homemade chocolate mousse to rival any French restaurant’s version and you can do it in 20 minutes.



Easiest Cinnamon Apple Tart

Sweet and crispy, this delicious apple tart consists of a layer of cinnamon-infused apples on top of a buttery, flaky crust. You’ll love these fall flavors so try this apple tart recipe with a cup of tea.
